A High Schooler's Guide to Adulting

Our website addresses the critical gap in education by providing high school students with essential resources on life topics rarely covered in traditional curricula. This website provides guidance on financial literacy, access to various opportunities, strategies for productivity management, and support for college/career prep.

About Us

We are a group of high schoolers who are passionate about helping young adults transition smoothly into adults. We hope to provide a resource for all students who are interested in learning more about adulting.
